Welcome to the 1980's


The cold war lit up like a roman candle, and it would burn up into Nuclear Warfare as the century came to an end.


History has changed drastically, as figures from the future appear throughout the 20th century, with knowledge of the future, and technology to come. These people would bring scientific advances to the past for their own personal gain, and bleed this timeline dry.


Corporations would bring insider trading to a whole new level, as many executives knew exactly where to invest, what to invest in, and how to form their plans to dominate the market, and the world.
